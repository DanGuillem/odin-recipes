Pasta Bake Recipe

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a tasty chicken pasta bake!

Ingredients:

  • Rigatoni Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • One red onion
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the onion, mushrooms and chicken in a pot
  2. Boil the pasta
  3. Once veg and chicken are cooked, add sauce
  4. Combine with cooked pasta
  5. Once mixed, place in an oven tray and top with cheese
  6. bake in oven for 10 mins at 200C or until crispy
